Common Elements / Themes

All of the selected films are adventure/fantasy films, meaning they have common themes of mystery and exploring. The clips I have selected all had a general dark and gloomy mood to them, with the lighting being dark and the music being ominous. Although the characters look vastly different, this common lighting and similar sound clips used allows the clips to smoothly merge together in order to make a single cohesive trailer. Furthermore, many of the clips used from all of the movies listed contain scenes of the characters either training or fighting, which is a common element that further allows for a smooth blending of the clips to make a cohesive project. As well as this, many of the consecutive clips contain similar themes, for example the combination of entrance clips at the end, and the combination of funeral/sad scenes in the middle. This usage of adjacent clips with similar themes (setting, music, etc.) further improves how well the clips go together allowing for an overall more cohesive project.
